National Park Visitor Count

National Parks are invaluable assets to the US Government for their reliable source and income, and analyzing tourism plays an undeniable hand in upholding that structure by giving insight on which parks prosper.

Created by: Curtis Lee and Mengguang Zou in collaboration with Ryan Brounley, Suresh Lodha, and Shobhit Maheshwari

Created for: CMPS165, Data Programming for Visualization, Fall 2016

Description:

A geomapping of National Parks and a graph to display visitor data.
